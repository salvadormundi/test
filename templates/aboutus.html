About Us

One of the things that makes any institution great is the number of friendships and memories that students create. Alumni associations are important since they help keep alumni connected with their school. They provide a forum for former students to share their experiences and collaborate on common goals, and helps create initiatives that unite the various classes of graduates. The strength of such association is established on the level of engagement and involvement of its members. Alumni associations are beneficial to all involved because they provide a support system for fellow graduates, present students, and the organizations they graduated from. EduRed is an Alumni Association for students and alumni likewise who can network and communicate to help each other succeed in the future.
